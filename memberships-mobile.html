Membership Notes

  • Exaltations, Shines and Reports available through Trine Lunar and Trine Venusian subscription tiers expire if they remain unused in their respective periods.
  • User-imposed limits on messaging still apply to members of the Trine Venusian subscription tier by way of our blocking and reporting functionality.
  • Priority in Matching, Messaging, and Browse between users in the same subscription tier is determined by the exact relative date and time their subscription became active.
  • Preview analysis of Mercury and Uranus is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Wednesdays to Sunrise local time on Thursdays.
  • Preview analysis of the Descendant, the Midheaven, and the Nadir is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Sundays to Sunrise local time on Mondays.
  • Preview analysis of Mars and Pluto is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Tuesdays to Sunrise local time on Wednesdays.
  • Preview analysis of Jupiter is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Thursdays to Sunrise local time on Fridays.
  • Preview analysis of Saturn and Chiron is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Saturdays to Sunrise local time on Sundays.
  • Preview analysis of Neptune and Lilith is available to all users, regardless of subscription status, from Sunrise local time on Fridays to Sunrise local time on Saturdays.
